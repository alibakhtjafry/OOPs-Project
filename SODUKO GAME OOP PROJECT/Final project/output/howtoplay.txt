Enter Row, Column, and Number (1-9 each):

Validation Rules:

Number must not be in the same row.
Number must not be in the same column.
Number must not be in the same 3x3 grid.

Making a Move:
Invalid moves will trigger a mistake sound and be disallowed.
Valid moves place the number and continue the game.
Complete the puzzle by following the rules.

Important Notes:
Mistakes count as blunders. Avoid them.
Check your progress and mistake count anytime.
Enjoy and challenge yourself to solve with fewest mistakes!

Technique:
Use TBM (Top Middle Bottom): A number in one grid's top appears in the middle of the next and 
the bottom of the third, and vice versa.
